## Unordered Lists: Use * or - for unordered lists.
### Use [ * ]
* Item 1
* Item 2
  * Subitem 1
  * Subitem 2
### Use [ - ]
- hypen 1
- hypen 2
    - sub-hypen-1
    - sub-hypen-2

## Ordered Lists: Use numbers (e.g., 1., 2.) for ordered lists.
### Use simple [ 1. , 2. , 3. ]
1. First item
2. Second item
   1. Subitem 1
   2. Subitem 2
